Getting Started

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click the "Start Free Trial" or "Sign Up" button
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Verify your email through the confirmation link we send
  4. Complete your profile with your name and preferences
  5. Add your first skill and start tracking!

You can start using the free plan immediately without entering any payment information.

After creating your account:

  1. Click "Add New Skill" from your dashboard
  2. Enter the name of the skill you want to master (e.g., "Guitar", "Programming", "Drawing")
  3. Optionally add a description and set your practice goals
  4. Choose a category and set reminder preferences
  5. Click "Create" and you're ready to start tracking!

The 10,000-hour rule, popularized by Malcolm Gladwell in his book "Outliers," suggests that it takes approximately 10,000 hours of deliberate practice to achieve mastery in any field.

While the exact number may vary depending on the skill and individual, the principle emphasizes the importance of consistent, focused practice over time. Our app helps you track and stay motivated throughout this journey.

Tracking Your Progress

Starting a practice session is easy:

  1. Go to your dashboard and select the skill you're practicing
  2. Click the large "Start Practice" button
  3. The timer will begin counting your practice time
  4. When you're done, click "Stop" and optionally add notes about your session

You can also pause and resume sessions if you need to take breaks.

Yes! If you forgot to track a session or practiced before creating your account:

  1. Click "Add Manual Entry" on your dashboard
  2. Select the skill, date, and duration
  3. Optionally add notes about what you practiced
  4. Click "Save" to add it to your history

Manual entries will be included in all your statistics and milestone calculations.

To edit or delete a practice session:

  1. Go to "Practice History" in your dashboard
  2. Find the session you want to modify
  3. Click the three-dot menu icon next to the session
  4. Choose "Edit" to modify details or "Delete" to remove it
  5. Confirm your changes

Your statistics will automatically update after any changes.

To get the most accurate tracking:

  • Track only focused practice: Don't count breaks, setup time, or casual playing
  • Start the timer when you begin: Make it a habit to hit "start" before you practice
  • Add session notes: Document what you worked on to track your progress
  • Be honest: Only count quality practice time toward your 10,000 hours
  • Sync regularly: Make sure your devices sync to avoid data loss

Features & Tools

Your dashboard includes several types of charts:

  • Progress Circle: Shows your overall progress toward 10,000 hours
  • Weekly Activity: Displays your practice time for each day of the week
  • Monthly Trends: Shows how your practice time changes over months
  • Session Length: Analyzes your typical session duration

Hover over any chart element to see detailed information. You can also switch between different time ranges using the date picker.

Set practice goals to stay on track:

  1. Go to Settings > Goals & Reminders
  2. Set your weekly or daily practice time goal

  4. Select your notification preferences (email, push, or both)

We'll send gentle reminders and celebrate when you hit your goals. You can adjust or disable reminders anytime.

Milestones are special celebrations for significant progress markers:

  • Hour Milestones: 100, 500, 1,000, 2,500, 5,000, and 10,000 hours
  • Streak Badges: 7, 30, 100, 365-day practice streaks
  • Consistency Awards: For maintaining regular practice habits
  • Special Achievements: First session, longest session, and more

View all your badges in the Achievements section of your profile.

Account Management

To update your profile:

  1. Click your profile picture in the top right corner
  2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  3. Navigate to "Profile" tab
  4. Edit your name, email, profile picture, and bio
  5. Click "Save Changes" when done

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings > Security
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter and confirm your new password
  5. Click "Update Password"

If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Customize your notifications in Settings > Notifications:

  • Practice Reminders: Daily or custom schedule
  • Milestone Alerts: When you hit achievements
  • Weekly Reports: Summary of your progress
  • Community Updates: Messages and interactions
  • Product Updates: New features and improvements

Choose email, push, or both for each type of notification.

Subscription & Billing

To upgrade your subscription:

  1. Go to Settings > Subscription
  2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
  3. Choose your desired plan (Beginner, Master, or Lifetime)
  4. Enter your payment information
  5. Confirm the upgrade

Your upgrade takes effect immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the current billing period.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your full payment details on our servers.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers to any paid plan. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days:

  1. Contact support at billing@10ktomastery.com
  2. Tell us you'd like a refund
  3. We'll process it within 5-7 business days

No questions asked! After 30 days, subscriptions are non-refundable, but you can cancel anytime to avoid future charges.

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to Settings > Subscription
  2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  3. Provide optional feedback about why you're leaving
  4. Confirm cancellation

You'll retain access to paid features until the end of your current billing period. Your data is saved, so you can reactivate anytime.

When you cancel:

  • All your practice data is safely stored
  • You can still access your account with free plan features
  • You can reactivate your subscription anytime
  • Your data is never deleted unless you explicitly request account deletion

If you downgrade to free, you'll only be able to track up to 3 skills, but all your data remains intact.

Troubleshooting

If your data isn't syncing between devices:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you're logged into the same account on all devices
  3. Force a manual sync by pulling down on the dashboard
  4. Try logging out and back in
  5. Update to the latest version of the app

If issues persist, contact support with your device details and we'll investigate.

If you can't log in:

  • Double-check your email and password for typos
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Check if your email address is verified

Still having issues? Contact support@10ktomastery.com with your registered email.

If your time tracking seems off:

  1. Check your Practice History for any duplicate entries
  2. Look for sessions that didn't stop properly (check for very long durations)
  3. Edit or delete any incorrect entries
  4. Make sure you're stopping sessions when you finish practicing

Your total hours will automatically recalculate after any edits.

10K to Mastery works best on modern browsers:

  • Firefox 88+
  • Safari 14+
  • Edge 90+

We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience and security.
