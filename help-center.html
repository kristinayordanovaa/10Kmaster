Getting Started

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click the "Get Started Free" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Verify your email and complete your profile
  4. Add your first skill and start tracking!

10K to Mastery is completely free - all features are available to everyone at no cost!

After creating your account:

  1. Click "+ Add New Skill" from your dashboard
  2. Enter the name of the skill you want to master (e.g., "Piano", "Web Development", "Spanish")
  3. Select a category icon (🎵 Music, 💻 Tech, 📚 Language, 🎨 Creative, 🏃 Sports)
  4. Choose an accent color for your progress visualization
  5. Click "Add Skill" and you're ready to start tracking!

The 10,000-hour rule, popularized by Malcolm Gladwell in his book "Outliers," suggests that it takes approximately 10,000 hours of deliberate practice to achieve mastery in any field.

While the exact number may vary depending on the skill and individual, the principle emphasizes the importance of consistent, focused practice over time. Our app helps you track and stay motivated throughout this journey.

Tracking Your Progress

Starting a practice session is easy:

  1. Go to your dashboard and find the skill you're practicing
  2. Click the "▶ Start Timer" button on the skill card
  3. The timer will begin counting your practice time in real-time
  4. When you're done, click "⏹ Stop Timer" and the time will automatically be added to your total hours

The timer displays in HH:MM:SS format and the skill card will highlight while the timer is active.

Yes! If you forgot to track a session or practiced before creating your account:

  • Find the skill on your dashboard
  • Click the "+ 30m" button to add 30 minutes
  • Or click the "+ 1h" button to add 1 hour
  • The time is instantly added to your skill's total hours

Manual entries will be included in all your statistics and milestone calculations. All time additions are recorded in your Time History.

To edit or delete a practice session:

  1. Click "⏱️ Time History" in the left sidebar
  2. Browse your practice sessions (filter by timeframe if needed)
  3. Find the session you want to modify
  4. Click the ✏️ edit icon to modify the time or click 🗑️ to delete it
  5. Confirm your changes

Your statistics and progress bars will automatically update after any changes.

To get the most accurate tracking:

  • Track only focused practice: Don't count breaks, setup time, or casual playing
  • Start the timer when you begin: Make it a habit to hit "▶ Start Timer" before you practice
  • Stop the timer when done: Always remember to click "⏹ Stop Timer" when finished
  • Use manual add sparingly: The +30m and +1h buttons are for quick entries, but the timer is more accurate
  • Be honest: Only count quality practice time toward your 10,000 hours

Features & Tools

Navigate to the Analytics section (📈) to view your practice data:

  • Practice Activity Heatmap: GitHub-style heatmap showing your daily practice intensity over weeks
  • Practice Breakdown by Skill: Bar chart showing how much time you've spent on each skill
  • Progress Bars: Each skill card shows a visual progress bar toward the 10,000-hour goal
  • Dashboard Stats: Quick overview showing Total Hours, Active Skills, Day Streak, and Hours This Week

Use the dropdown menus to adjust timeframes and view different periods. Darker colors in the heatmap indicate more practice hours that day.

Your practice statistics are displayed in multiple places:

  • Dashboard Overview: See Total Hours, Active Skills, Current Streak, and Hours This Week at the top
  • Skill Cards: Each skill shows its hours, progress percentage, and a visual progress bar

  • Time History: Review all your logged practice sessions with dates and durations

All statistics update in real-time as you track practice sessions.

  • Special: Early Bird, Night Owl, Weekend Warrior, Holiday Dedication, Perfect Week

Each achievement shows your progress with a progress bar and is marked with a checkmark when completed.

Account Management

To update your profile:

  1. Click "⚙️ Settings" in the left sidebar
  2. In the Profile section, you can update:
    • Your avatar (choose from 16 available options)
    • Display name
    • Email address
    • Bio/About section
  3. Click "Save Changes" when done

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings (⚙️) > Security tab
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter and confirm your new password
  5. Click "Update Password"

If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to receive a reset email.

You can customize your notifications in Settings > Notifications:

  • Milestone Alerts: Celebrate when you hit major achievements and hour milestones
  • Achievement Unlocks: Get notified when you earn new badges
  • Progress Updates: Receive summaries of your practice activity

You can enable or disable each notification type independently. Notifications help keep you motivated and on track toward your 10,000-hour goals.

Troubleshooting

10K to Mastery is currently a web-based application. Your data is automatically saved as you use the app. If you're experiencing issues:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure you're logged into your account
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  5. Try a different browser to isolate the issue

If issues persist, contact support with details about what you're experiencing and which browser you're using.

If you can't log in:

  • Double-check your email and password for typos
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Check if your email address is verified

Still having issues? Contact support@10ktomastery.com with your registered email.

If your time tracking seems off:

  1. Check your Practice History for any duplicate entries
  2. Look for sessions that didn't stop properly (check for very long durations)
  3. Edit or delete any incorrect entries
  4. Make sure you're stopping sessions when you finish practicing

Your total hours will automatically recalculate after any edits.

10K to Mastery works best on modern browsers:

  • Chrome 90+ (recommended)
  • Firefox 88+
  • Safari 14+
  • Edge 90+

We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience and security.

Yes! You own your data and can export it anytime:

  1. Go to Settings (⚙️) > Data & Privacy tab
  2. Click "Export My Data"
  3. Choose your export format (CSV for spreadsheets, JSON for developers, or PDF for reports)
  4. Click "Generate Export"
  5. Download your file when ready (usually within a few minutes)

Your export will include all your skills, practice sessions, total hours, statistics, and achievement progress.

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Export your data first (this is irreversible!)
  2. Go to Settings > Data & Privacy
  3. Scroll to "Delete Account"
  4. Enter your password to confirm
  5. Click "Permanently Delete My Account"

Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
