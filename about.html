About 10 to Master

Empowering people to achieve excellence through deliberate practice and consistent tracking

Our Story

10 to Master was founded by a group of passionate learners who struggled to track their progress while mastering various skills. From learning musical instruments to coding, painting to public speaking, we experienced firsthand how difficult it is to stay motivated over the long journey to expertise.

We realized that while many apps track habits or time, none specifically focused on the long-term journey to mastery. So we built one. A platform designed to celebrate your progress, provide meaningful insights, and keep you motivated through every milestone on your 10,000-hour journey.

Our Mission

We believe that mastery is achievable for anyone willing to put in the time and effort. Based on Malcolm Gladwell's famous 10,000-hour rule, we created a platform that makes tracking your journey to mastery simple, motivating, and insightful.

Our mission is to help people around the world achieve their full potential by providing the tools, insights, and motivation needed to stay committed to their craft.
